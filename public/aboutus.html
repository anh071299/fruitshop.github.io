Small Story

Organic since 1995

Starting a farm

Because of our enthusiasm for organic food, we immediately began cultivating the land to grow food for family. As our garden produced more than enough food to nourish our family, we began selling the organic produce to local food store and other retailers. We took on a small parcel of land and this is the first step on a long journey towards us becoming a producer ourselves. The land is now certified organic

The Best Organic in the town

We expanded our land that specializes in vegetable, fruit, milk... The farm became certified organic in 2008, making it one of the oldest certified organic farms. Fresh Farm supplies many of the top restaurants and retailers in the area.The farm is very committed to giving back to the community, so any excess food is donated to local food banks and churches.

Store Opening

We opened our first shop and received enthusiastic support from customers. Now we have 3 main stores in 3 regions and authorized dealers. The shop is open daily from 9am to 6pm. We often have discount items for our loyal customers

Online Shop and Shipment

We move the farm into the “digital age” with a website, a social media presence, podcast interviews and, most recently, Fresh Farm online store. The farm offers an online store that not only sells our produce, but sources eggs, honey, cheese and more from local farmers.
